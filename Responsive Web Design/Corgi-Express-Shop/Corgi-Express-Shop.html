Corgis have a big appetite!

These little guys like to eat. A LOT. In order to keep them healthy, you'll have to keep them active, or they'll become real chonky real soon!
Remember that a little bit of chonkyness is cute and all, but you want to make sure your doggo remains healthy.

These doggos love to play.

Maybe it's from the sugar high these guys get from all the food they eat, but corgis LOVE to play. They love to run around, jump around, and play around. So, if you are looking to get one, make sure you carve out some time in your daily schedule to play with the little guy and take him out for a walk.

They may be smol, but they are also big floofs.

Corgi's coats have two layers: an outer layer that is course and protects them from the elements, and an inner layer that is soft and light. Because they are such floofs, they need to be brushed daily and should be given extra baths during shedding season.

Corgos live on average for 60 human years!

Corgis have a lifespan averaging between 11 and 13 years. They are considered seniors after becoming 8. Make sure you keep your pup healthy so that he may enjoy his later years too!
